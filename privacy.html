6. P5X Gacha Pull Tracker (가챠 통계) Data

The Pull Tracker feature lets you fetch and visualize your P5X gacha history using a user-supplied URL. For technical reasons (CORS), the request is proxied via iant.kr. The following clarifies what is and is not processed/stored.

What We Process

  • The URL you paste (temporary request URL issued by the game) is used only to fetch your gacha history.
  • The resulting gacha history records (dates, item names/grades) are handled client-side and may be merged for statistics.
  • No account identifiers (e.g., UID/auth key) are stored by this site. The temporary key is not saved on our servers.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Local (in your browser)

  • We store recent inputs and merged results in browser storage to speed up future visits (e.g., pull-tracker:last-response, pull-tracker:merged, pull-tracker:hide4).

Google Drive (optional)

  • If you sign in and choose to sync, the merged history is saved in your Google Drive AppDataFolder as p5x_pull_tracker.json.
  • We request the minimal scope (drive.appdata) and access only this app-specific file. You can delete it at any time.

How We Use Data

  • Data is used to compute personal statistics (rates, averages, “in progress” pity) and to render charts locally in your browser.
  • Aggregated/anonymous statistics may be used for analysis at iant.kr/gacha. We do not publish personally identifiable information.

How To Delete

  • Use the Clear button on the Pull Tracker page to wipe local data and request deletion of the Drive file.
  • You may also manually remove the file from Google Drive AppDataFolder and clear your browser storage/cookies.

Security Notes

  • Google sign-in is handled by Google Identity Services. Access tokens are stored only in your browser and are not kept on our servers.
  • We do not request access to your general Drive files; only the app’s hidden AppData file is used when you opt-in.
