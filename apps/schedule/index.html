Release Schedule

This schedule is for GLB/JP servers. For SEA server, add +1 week to each date.
Until version 4.0, new characters release every 2 weeks; after 4.0, the interval changes to 3 weeks. Schedule may vary due to events/holidays. Character release order is subject to change.
For detailed patch history, see P5X Patch History by Sages.
